Software development life cycle

Phase Description
Idea Phase The idea of the product is being discussed with the team so basically brainstorming
Planning Phase The plan is being made so basically the blueprint of the idea
Analyzing Phase Analyzing the plan to see if tweaks need to be made
Testing phase Testing the product to see if it works or needs fixing.
Maintenance Phase Fixing or editing or debugging the product to make it better.
Deployment Phase The product is out there in the world for users to use.
